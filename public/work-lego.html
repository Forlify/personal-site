Save the bricks!



One of my challenging projects that required creative approach and special solutions was made for Ideo Bricks — company that specializes in making models of buildings and historical monuments using LEGO bricks. The goal was to create a program that helps to build pictures using less LEGO bricks. The program helped to minimize production cost of each item, that ultimately had a positive impact on company’s income.
